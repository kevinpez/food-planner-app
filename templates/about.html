About BellySattva

An AI-powered nutrition tracking application that helps you make healthier food choices.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to make informed nutrition decisions through intelligent food tracking, barcode scanning, and personalized AI recommendations.

How It Works

  • Scan UPC barcodes for instant food recognition
  • Track daily nutrition with detailed analytics
  • Get AI-powered meal recommendations
  • Plan meals and monitor progress

Data Sources

  • Open Food Facts: Comprehensive food database
  • Anthropic Claude: AI meal recommendations
  • User Input: Custom food entries

Privacy & Security

Your data is secure with industry-standard encryption, CSRF protection, and no sharing with third parties. You own your nutrition data.
